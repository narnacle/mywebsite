🏆 Hogwarts Legacy 100% Completion Guide

100% completing Hogwarts Legacy means fully finishing all main story content, side quests, collectibles, challenges, and trophies/achievements. Here's a detailed checklist to help you achieve that:

🎓 MAIN STORY (Required for 100%)

  • Complete all 47 main quests
  • Choose a house (any house works)
  • Finish the final story quest ("The Final Repository")

🧭 SIDE CONTENT

✅ Side Quests (100% = 57 total)

  • All relationship quests (Poppy, Sebastian, Natsai)
  • Miscellaneous quests found in villages, Hogwarts, and Hogsmeade

✅ Assignments

  • Complete all 12 Professor Assignments to unlock key spells

🗺️ EXPLORATION & COLLECTIBLES

📚 Field Guide Pages (100% = 236 total)

  • Hogwarts: 150 pages
  • Hogsmeade: 55 pages
  • World Map: 31 pages

🧩 Merlin Trials (100% = 95 trials)

  • Required for max inventory space

🏰 Demiguise Statues (100% = 30 total)

  • Needed to upgrade Alohomora to level 3

🎒 Collection Items

  • Traits, Gear Appearances, Ingredients, etc.

🐲 Beasts

  • Rescue and breed all 13 beast types
  • Complete the Vivarium upgrades

🧙‍♂️ TALENTS & LEVELING

🔓 Reach Level 40

  • Max level in the game

🧪 Unlock All Talents

  • You can’t unlock every talent in one playthrough (max is ~36 of 48), so focus on what you need for trophies

🏆 TROPHIES & ACHIEVEMENTS

Some key ones include:

  • The Map Chamber – Finish the main story
  • The Nature of the Beast – Breed every beast
  • The Intrepid Explorer – Discover all Floo Flames
  • Rise to the Challenges – Complete all challenges (combat, exploration, etc.)
  • A Forte for Achievement – Reach Level 40
  • The Gryffindor in the Grave / etc. – Each House has a unique quest
    • ❗ You need to do a partial 4 playthroughs (or backup save trick) to get the house-specific trophies

📋 CHALLENGES (from Field Guide Menu)

There are 5 categories to complete:

  1. Combat
  2. Quests
  3. Exploration
  4. Field Guide Pages
  5. Room of Requirement

Complete every tier in each category.

🛏️ ROOM OF REQUIREMENT

  • Fully decorate/customize the Room
  • Unlock all Vivarium types
  • Rescue and care for all beasts
  • Collect all Conjurations

💡 TIPS FOR 100%:

  • Use Revelio constantly
  • Fly with the broom to reveal hidden Field Guide Pages and Trials
  • Use the map filters to track collectibles
  • Backup saves for House-specific trophies
